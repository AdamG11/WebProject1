Welcome to Motion Fitness Gym Sligo

Motion Fitness Gym Sligo is Leading the way with affordable Gym Memberships, Classes and Tailored Personal Training with some of the top Trainers around the country to help you achieve results you never thought possible. Motion Fitness Gym is located in the heart of Sligo Town.

Here at Motion Fitness Gym, we warmly welcome people of all ages and fitness levels. Whether you are a beginner in the gym, or an experienced fitness guru, we have top of the line equipment dedicated to your fitness needs, ranging from body building to those who are sport athletes and everything inbetween.

Due to our Large Facility, top line equipment and excellent trainers, we here at Motion fitness, regularly invite local sorts clubs to help improve their strength, conditioning and sport performance which will allow each athlete to grow and reach their potential, while also improving their physical and mental preperation and approach to games. The athletes involved will also be taught the principles of sports nutrition and how to get the best out of their diet to aid them in training, recovery and games.

Our goal is to make you the best person you want to be physically aswell as mentally in a friendly, modern environment.

Since we were established in 2018, Motion Fitness has changed lives all aorund Sligo. Above all, we have made it our mission to teach the world how to build strength, character and spirit through focused fitness. Whether you are new to this or a master of the craft, Motion Fitness can help wash away the stresses and shape a stronger, better you.
